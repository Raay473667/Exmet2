Why EDS Cannot detect light elements?
Light elements (Z < 11) cannot be routinely analysed by EDS. Hydrogen (Z = 1) and He (Z = 2) do not have Characteristic X-rays, and the Li (Z = 3) K X-rays are of too low energy to be detected by EDS.
Lithium and beryllium, meanwhile, have sufficiently low atomic numbers that the energy of X-rays given off by Li or Be samples is insufficient for measurement, and often times they cannot be tested as a result.
